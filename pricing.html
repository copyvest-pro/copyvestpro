Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before investing.

How does CopyVestPro work?
CopyVestPro is a copy trading platform that automatically mirrors the trades of our verified expert traders into your account. You invest, we trade on your behalf using proven strategies, and your returns are credited automatically at the end of your contract period.
When do I receive my returns?
Returns are credited automatically at the end of your plan contract duration — 14, 60, 99, or 365 days depending on your chosen plan. Withdrawals are processed instantly once requested.
What payment methods are accepted?
We primarily accept Bitcoin (BTC) and Tether (USDT) for all deposits and withdrawals due to their global accessibility and near-instant processing speeds. Additional payment options may be available depending on your region.
Is CopyVestPro regulated and licensed?
Yes. CopyVestPro is a registered and licensed trading company authorized to operate globally. We comply fully with all applicable financial regulations and maintain strict standards of client fund protection.
How does the referral programme work?
Every investor receives a unique referral code. When someone signs up and invests using your code, you earn 5% of their invested amount as a bonus. There is no limit to how many referrals you can make.
What happens if I want to withdraw early?
Early withdrawals are subject to our standard terms and conditions. We recommend completing the full contract duration to receive your maximum ROI. Please contact our support team for specific early withdrawal enquiries.
